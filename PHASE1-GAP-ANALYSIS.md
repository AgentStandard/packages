## Gaps Identified

### Career Gaps (CRITICAL — most of what we have is lifestyle)

**1. interview-coach** — No interview prep package exists.
- job-hunt-agent tracks applications but doesn't coach conversations
- Massive, recurring, high-value use case
- Every job seeker eventually faces an interview

**2. negotiation-coach** — salary-scout knows market value but doesn't coach the actual conversation.
- Raise requests, promotion talks, job offer negotiation are three separate but related pain points
- High emotional stakes = high agent stickiness
- Different from salary-scout (benchmarks) — this is tactics and conversation prep

**3. manager-coach** — No management/leadership package.
- "I just got promoted, now what?" is one of the most common professional crises
- 1:1s, giving hard feedback, managing poor performers, navigating up — all underprovided
- Experienced managers need it too (politics, difficult stakeholders, culture)

**4. career-pivot** — No "I want to change careers" package.
- Enormous underserved market — millions of people mid-reinvention
- Requires: transferable skills mapping, gap analysis, pivot roadmap, network bridge strategy
- Distinct from idea-validator (which is about a business idea) and job-hunt-agent (which assumes you know what you want)

### Lifestyle / Emotional Gaps

**5. relationship-coach** — No emotional support / relationship navigation package.
- Camille's use case: dumped workplace woes onto the bot and loved it
- Not therapy. Not cheerleading. The thoughtful friend who asks the right questions.
- Covers: friendships, romantic relationships, workplace dynamics, family — all through the lens of "what do you actually want here?"

**6. dream-decoder** — No dream interpretation package.
- Direct request from Jackson's parents on the call
- Fun, low-stakes, viral — ideal for casual users who don't have a "burning question"
- Distinct from dream-audit (which is about creative goals, not sleep dreams)

### Life Stage Gap

**7. retiree-navigator** — No retirement / life transition package.
- Direct request from Jackson's parents — their own pain point
- Medicare navigation, leaving the workforce, retirement income planning, senior health decisions
- Enormous market (70m+ Baby Boomers in US alone)
- Very low AI competition in this specific format
