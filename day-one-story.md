# Day One Story

> Raw material for marketing copy, ProductHunt launch, Reddit post, investor narrative.

---

08:00 — shower thought. "What if this wasn't so hard?"

By midnight:

- Live Telegram bot on a production server in London
- 4-question onboarding that seeds a personalised agent with your name, your rules, your current situation
- 30-package consumer catalogue built by an AI scout scanning the ecosystem
- A real domain (agentstandard.ai)
- A real site, mobile-responsive, with a two-tone hero and a manifesto
- Legal compliance — Terms, Privacy Policy, ICO registered
- GitHub org with certification standard, contributing guidelines, community discussions
- Supabase upvote counters, community package pages
- A strategic framework that answers every question a VC would ask

And I didn't write a line of code.

---

## Notes for copy use

- The "shower thought to production server" arc is the hook
- "And I didn't write a line of code" is the close — lands hardest at the end
- The list should stay dense — the volume is the point
- Audience: ProductHunt, Reddit r/SideProject, Twitter/X launch thread, investor deck slide
- Tone: matter-of-fact, not braggy. Let the list do the work.
