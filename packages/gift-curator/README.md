# Gift Curator

> Thoughtful gifts. Every time.

**Vertical:** Social  
**Telegram:** Yes  
**Status:** Pending certification

## About

Learns the preferences of people in your life over time. When an occasion comes up, suggests gifts that genuinely fit.

## Try it

**On Telegram (zero setup):**  
[Start Gift Curator on Telegram](https://t.me/AgentStandardAI_bot?start=gift-curator)

**On OpenClaw:**
1. Copy the `telegram_system_prompt` from `agentstandard.json`
2. Paste into your agent's `SOUL.md`
3. Restart your agent
