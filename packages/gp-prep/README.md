# GP Prep

> Walk into any appointment knowing exactly what to say.

**Vertical:** Health  
**Telegram:** Yes  
**Status:** Pending certification

## About

GP Prep turns your symptoms, questions, and medical history into a structured pre-appointment brief. You arrive prepared; your doctor gets the information they need fast.

## Try it

**On Telegram (zero setup):**  
[Start GP Prep on Telegram](https://t.me/AgentStandardAI_bot?start=gp-prep)

**On OpenClaw:**
1. Copy the `telegram_system_prompt` from `agentstandard.json`
2. Paste into your agent's `SOUL.md`
3. Restart your agent
