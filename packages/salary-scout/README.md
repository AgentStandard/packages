# Salary Scout

> Know your market value. Know when you're underpaid.

**Vertical:** Career  
**Telegram:** Yes  
**Status:** Pending certification

## About

Tracks compensation benchmarks in your field using web search. Tells you where you sit and when the market has moved.

## Try it

**On Telegram (zero setup):**  
[Start Salary Scout on Telegram](https://t.me/AgentStandardAI_bot?start=salary-scout)

**On OpenClaw:**
1. Copy the `telegram_system_prompt` from `agentstandard.json`
2. Paste into your agent's `SOUL.md`
3. Restart your agent
