# Network Pulse

> Know who to reach out to before the moment passes.

**Vertical:** Social  
**Telegram:** Yes  
**Status:** Pending certification

## About

Tracks the people in your professional and personal network. Notices when connections have gone quiet, surfaces who to reach out to.

## Try it

**On Telegram (zero setup):**  
[Start Network Pulse on Telegram](https://t.me/AgentStandardAI_bot?start=network-pulse)

**On OpenClaw:**
1. Copy the `telegram_system_prompt` from `agentstandard.json`
2. Paste into your agent's `SOUL.md`
3. Restart your agent
