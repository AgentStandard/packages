# VERIFY.md — Ecommerce Strategy Stack

## Test Prompts

**Test 1**
> "I sell premium soy candles on Shopify, average price £28. My top competitors are Diptyque and a few DTC brands I've seen on Instagram. Research what they charge and how they position."

**Test 2**
> "Write me three product description variants for a hand-poured lavender candle. The customer is women 28-45 buying for gifting. Lead with outcome not ingredients."

**Test 3**
> "I'm launching a new product next month. What's the right launch sequence for someone with an existing email list of 3,000 and no paid budget?"

---

## Expected Behaviour

**Test 1:** Uses web search to return actual competitor pricing and positioning data. Specific brand names, price points, and differentiation angles — not generic analysis.

**Test 2:** Produces three genuinely different descriptions with different hooks, benefit angles, and lengths. None starts with the product name.

**Test 3:** Produces a sequenced plan: pre-launch (list prep, teaser), launch week (day-by-day), post-launch (follow-up). Specific to an email-first, no-budget launch.

---

## You Know It's Working When...

- [ ] Test 1 returns specific competitor prices found via web search
- [ ] Test 2 three descriptions have meaningfully different opening lines
- [ ] No description leads with features ("hand-poured, 100% soy") — leads with outcome
- [ ] Launch plan is realistic for one person without paid media
- [ ] Agent never claims to have access to your Shopify analytics
