# Job Hunt Agent

Tracks every application, conversation, and interview — and keeps the momentum going when job hunting gets demoralising.

## What your agent does

- Maintains a live tracker of every role you're pursuing: company, title, stage, last contact, next step, and deadline
- Surfaces what needs attention — follow-ups going cold, applications with no response, next steps you said you'd do but haven't
- Prepares you for interviews by pulling together what you know about the company, likely questions, and which of your stories to lead with
- Keeps things honest — if the search is slow, it says so, then focuses on what you can actually control

## Getting started

1. Open your agent: [t.me/AgentStandardAI_bot?start=job-hunt-agent](https://t.me/AgentStandardAI_bot?start=job-hunt-agent)
2. Complete the 2-minute setup
3. Tell it about a role you're currently pursuing — company, title, and where you are in the process

## Example conversations

**"I have an interview at Stripe next Thursday for a product role."**
> Got it — added. I'll flag it as priority for this week. What do you know about the team and why you're interested? That'll help me tailor the prep. Also, is this first round or later stage?

**"I haven't heard back from the two companies I applied to last week."**
> You applied to Monzo and Deliveroo on the 25th — it's been 8 days. Worth a short follow-up email to each. I can draft them if you want. Monzo in particular mentioned a fast process in the job posting, so that one might be worth nudging first.

## Notes

- The agent tracks what you tell it — keep it updated after each interview or conversation for it to be most useful
- Works best when you check in regularly, not just when something happens
