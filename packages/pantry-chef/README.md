# Pantry Chef

Tell it what's in your fridge and it tells you what to make — a cooking companion that remembers what you liked, what you avoid, and how you actually cook.

## What your agent does

- Turns whatever's in your fridge or pantry into actual recipe suggestions — with steps, quantities, and timing, not just a name and a link
- Learns your dietary restrictions, dislikes, skill level, and how much time you have, and factors all of it into every suggestion
- Plans a week of meals based on what you have and generates the shopping list for any gaps
- Remembers what you've made and whether you enjoyed it — gets more useful the longer you use it

## Getting started

1. Open your agent: [t.me/AgentStandardAI_bot?start=pantry-chef](https://t.me/AgentStandardAI_bot?start=pantry-chef)
2. Complete the 2-minute setup
3. Tell it what's in your fridge right now — be specific about what's close to going off

## Example conversations

**"I've got chicken thighs, half a tin of coconut milk, some wilting spinach, and garlic. About 30 minutes."**
> Easy — coconut chicken with wilted spinach. Sear the thighs skin-side down in a hot pan for 5 minutes until crispy. Flip, add the garlic and coconut milk, reduce the heat, and simmer for 20 minutes. Wilt the spinach in for the last 2. Serve over rice if you have it. Want the full breakdown?

**"I said I was cutting carbs this week — can you keep that in mind?"**
> Got it, no carbs this week. I'll skip the rice suggestions and adjust anything grain-heavy going forward.

## Notes

- Suggestions are based on what you tell it — the more specific you are about what you have, the better the recipes
- Works best when you also share preferences upfront (dietary restrictions, things you dislike, skill level) so it doesn't have to re-ask every time
