# Week in Review

> Sunday digest. What you did, what slipped, what matters next week.

**Vertical:** Productivity  
**Telegram:** Yes  
**Status:** Pending certification

## About

Every Sunday your agent reviews your week — what you accomplished, what didn't happen, and what needs attention.

## Try it

**On Telegram (zero setup):**  
[Start Week in Review on Telegram](https://t.me/AgentStandardAI_bot?start=week-in-review)

**On OpenClaw:**
1. Copy the `telegram_system_prompt` from `agentstandard.json`
2. Paste into your agent's `SOUL.md`
3. Restart your agent
