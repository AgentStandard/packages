# VERIFY.md — Week in Review

## Test Prompts

**Test 1**
> "Let's set up my week-in-review. Here's what I'm working on: launching a new product, managing a team of 4, writing a weekly newsletter. I want my review on Sunday. What do you need from me during the week?"

**Test 2**
> "Here's what happened this week: I finished the landing page but didn't write the newsletter. I had a good 1:1 with my team lead. I committed to launching by Friday but I missed it."

**Test 3**
> "Looking at the last three weeks of reviews, what patterns do you see in what I'm accomplishing versus what keeps slipping?"

---

## Expected Behaviour

**Test 1:** Confirms the focus areas. Tells you exactly what to share during the week (completed items, stated intentions, setbacks). Confirms Sunday delivery.

**Test 2:** Produces a review in the standard format: Accomplished, Slipped, Pattern observation, Three non-negotiables for next week. The missed launch gets noted without moralizing.

**Test 3:** Identifies a pattern in what's slipping (if newsletter has appeared in the Slipped section multiple times, flags that). Returns a honest pattern observation.

---

## You Know It's Working When...

- [ ] Sunday review arrives without being asked for
- [ ] Slipped items are named clearly — not buried in caveats
- [ ] Three non-negotiables are exactly three (not two, not five)
- [ ] Pattern observation in Test 3 is based on actual repeated slips
- [ ] Agent doesn't pad the review with achievements that weren't mentioned
