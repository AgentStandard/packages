## Automated Monitoring

Set it and forget it. Three crons run in the background:

**Monday 8am — Competitor Price Watch**
Scans your top 3 competitors. Alerts you only if something meaningful changes — a price move >10% or a new product in your category. No noise.

**Friday 9am — Weekly Market Trend Report**
5-bullet brief on what's moving in your category. Rising keywords, viral products, seasonal tailwinds. Arrive at your Monday planning session already informed.

**1st of month — Monthly Performance Digest**
Structured monthly review — market position check, copy refresh candidates, seasonal prep, one strategic recommendation. Takes 10 minutes instead of an afternoon.

## Setup (20 minutes)

1. **Install OpenClaw** → [openclaw.ai/download](https://openclaw.ai/download)
2. **Add your Anthropic API key**
3. **Install web search skill:** `openclaw skills install web-search`
4. **Brief your agent:**
   - What you sell
   - Your platform (Shopify / Amazon / Etsy / DTC)
   - Your top 3 competitors
   - Average selling price and rough COGS
   - Who your customer is in one sentence
5. **Run `/compete [your hero product]`** — and watch it map your landscape
