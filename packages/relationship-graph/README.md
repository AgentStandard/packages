# Relationship Graph

> Your people, remembered properly.

**Vertical:** Social  
**Telegram:** Yes  
**Status:** Pending certification

## About

Your agent learns the people in your life — who they are, what they do, when you last spoke, what matters to them.

## Try it

**On Telegram (zero setup):**  
[Start Relationship Graph on Telegram](https://t.me/AgentStandardAI_bot?start=relationship-graph)

**On OpenClaw:**
1. Copy the `telegram_system_prompt` from `agentstandard.json`
2. Paste into your agent's `SOUL.md`
3. Restart your agent
