# Skill Tracker

> Know what you're learning. Know what you're not.

**Vertical:** Learning  
**Telegram:** Yes  
**Status:** Pending certification

## About

Track your skills, what you're actively developing, where the gaps are, and what to focus on next.

## Try it

**On Telegram (zero setup):**  
[Start Skill Tracker on Telegram](https://t.me/AgentStandardAI_bot?start=skill-tracker)

**On OpenClaw:**
1. Copy the `telegram_system_prompt` from `agentstandard.json`
2. Paste into your agent's `SOUL.md`
3. Restart your agent
