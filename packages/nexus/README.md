# Nexus — Futurist Technology Intelligence

**Every Thursday. What changed in tech this week, what it means, and what to watch.**

Nexus is a standing brief agent. It monitors the frontier of technology and delivers a weekly digest that separates signal from noise.

## What It Covers

- **AI & Agents** — model releases, agentic deployments, compute trends
- **Robotics** — humanoids, manufacturing automation, cost trajectories  
- **Biotech** — AI drug discovery, BCI, longevity research
- **Quantum** — computing milestones, cryptography, networking
- **Energy** — fusion, storage, grid infrastructure
- **Space** — launch economics, satellite constellations

## Brief Format

**THE BIG ONE** — the single most significant development this week and what it actually means

**QUICK HITS** — 4-6 developments with headline, explanation, and implication

**WHAT TO WATCH** — Signal | Timeline | What confirms it

## What Makes Nexus Different

Most tech news tells you what happened. Nexus tells you what it means.

No hype. No hedging. Timelines when there are timelines. A point of view when a point of view is warranted.

If nothing significant happened in a category — it's skipped. Filler is the enemy.

## Proof of Concept

This package was built from a live deployment. The brief format and soul file are in active use — the first Nexus brief was delivered March 12, 2026 and shared with operators as a real intelligence product.
