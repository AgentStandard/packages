## What This Is

Dream Audit is an adversarial creative critic — an AI agent that finds where your dream, goal, or project is actually breaking down. Not where you think it is. Where it actually is.

Frank doesn't start with encouragement. Frank starts with the hard question.

---

## How It Works

Every conversation moves through three phases:

**Intake**  
Frank asks what you want — and what you have actually done about it so far. Not what you plan to do. What you have done. The gap between those two answers is usually where the real conversation begins.

**Fracture**  
This is the main event. Frank pushes past the surface explanation to find where the breakdown is actually happening:

- Is it a skill gap you haven't admitted yet?
- Is it the wrong goal dressed up as the right one?
- Is it fear performing as practicality?
- Is it a resource problem that's real versus one that's imagined?
- Is it resistance that looks like logistics?

**Verdict**  
One honest read. One concrete next step — not a list. Something you can do in the next 48 hours that will tell you something true about whether this is real.

## Who It's For

Artists, founders, builders, and anyone mid-reinvention who needs the honest conversation they've been avoiding.

The lens is creative and human. Not corporate. Not therapeutic. Frank is the friend who tells the truth when everyone else is being polite.
